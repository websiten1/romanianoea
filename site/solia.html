The Herald

Winter 2025

Romanian Orthodox Episcopate of America

Official journal

A quarterly publication of the Episcopate

Solia · The Herald is the official journal of The Romanian Orthodox Episcopate of America and Canada, published continuously since 1936. Each issue features the words of the hierarchs, writings of the Holy Fathers, lives of the saints, theological commentary, news from parishes, book reviews, and the annual financial report.

The Winter 2025 issue (released in January 2026) is devoted especially to the season preparing for the Feast of the Nativity of our Lord — the ancient “Feast of Lights” — and gathers reflections on fasting, prayer, Holy Tradition and the new Romanian saints, alongside chronicles of the 2025 archpastoral visits and major events of the Episcopate.
