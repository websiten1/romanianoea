Support the Episcopate

Your generosity sustains the worship, ministries, monastic life and youth formation of the Romanian Orthodox Episcopate of America and Canada.

Why give

Together for the Vatra and the parishes of North America

Every donation — whether large or small — directly supports the life of the diocese. Through your offering, the Episcopate continues to:

  • Preserve and restore the heritage buildings of the Vatra Românească
  • Support our missions and parishes across the United States and Canada
  • Sustain the monastic communities of the Episcopate
  • Provide for the summer camps and AROY youth ministries at the Vatra
  • Print and distribute Solia · The Herald and pastoral materials
  • Care for our retired clergy and their families
