    Directions

    Finding the Vatra

    From the East

    Follow the Ohio Turnpike to Exit 59; take US-23 North toward Ann Arbor until you reach I-94. Take I-94 West toward Chicago to Exit 150 (Grass Lake). Turn left (south) onto Mt Hope Rd. Continue about 2 miles and turn right (west) at the stop light onto Michigan Ave. Pass through the village of Grass Lake and turn left (south) onto Wolf Lake Rd (Frank’s Shop-Rite on the corner). Continue about 1 mile and turn right (west) onto Page Ave. Go approximately 1/4 mile and turn left (south) onto Grey Tower Rd. The Chancery and Vatra Românească are about 1/2 mile down the road on the left.

    From the West

    Follow I-94 East to Exit 142 (US-127 South – Hudson). Follow US-127 South to the second exit (Page Ave). Turn left (east) onto Page Ave, pass through the town of Michigan Center, and continue for approximately 6.5 miles. Turn right (south) onto Grey Tower Rd. If you reach the end of Page Ave (Wolf Lake Rd), you’ve gone 1/4 mile too far.
