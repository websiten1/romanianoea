Summer Camps at the Vatra · 2025

Another season of prayer, catechesis, sport and lifelong friendship at the Vatra Românească.

Summer 2025 · Grass Lake, Michigan

Four camps, one Vatra

Throughout the summer of 2025, the Vatra Românească welcomed hundreds of children, teens and young adults from parishes across the United States and Canada for its four annual camp programs:

  • Camp Vatra Mici (ages 8–12)
  • Camp Vatra Mari (ages 13–17)
  • Saint Anthony the Great Camp — for young men (18+)
  • Saint Mary Camp — for young women (18+)

A day at the Vatra

The camps keep a steady rhythm: morning prayers in the chapel, catechesis led by visiting clergy and monastics, classes on Romanian Orthodox heritage, sports and swimming in the afternoon, folk dancing, songs and campfires in the evening — and the Divine Liturgy at the heart of each week.

Highlights from 2025

  • Hierarchical Divine Liturgy with His Eminence Archbishop Nathaniel and His Grace Bishop Andrei
  • Catechesis on the lives of the saints and the meaning of the Divine Liturgy
  • New counselors-in-training from AROY parish chapters
  • A special afternoon of service work on the Vatra grounds
  • Traditional sports tournaments and the much-loved talent night

Scholarships & registration

No child should miss camp for financial reasons. Scholarships are available through parish priests and through the Episcopate's Youth Department. Registration for the next season opens each spring; details are published through Solia · The Herald and on the Episcopate website.

AROY — year-round

Many campers go home and continue the life they began at camp through their parish AROY chapter. AROY hosts its flagship Labor Day Conference & Tournament in rotating host parishes each year.
