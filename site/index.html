His Eminence

Archbishop Nathaniel

Archbishop of Detroit and the Romanian Orthodox Episcopate of America and Canada

Archbishop Nathaniel leads the Episcopate from the administrative center at the Vatra Românească in Grass Lake, Michigan. Under his archpastoral guidance, the diocese serves Romanian-American Orthodox faithful and missions in the United States and Canada, in canonical communion with the Orthodox Church in America.

His Grace Bishop Andrei (Hoarște) serves as Auxiliary (Vicar) Bishop, ordained February 1, 2025.
