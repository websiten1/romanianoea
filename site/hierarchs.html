His Eminence

Archbishop Nathaniel — a brief biographical note

For decades, Archbishop Nathaniel has shepherded the Romanian Orthodox Episcopate of America and Canada from its administrative center at the Vatra Românească in Grass Lake, Michigan. Under his archpastoral guidance, the diocese has welcomed new parishes and missions, ordained new clergy, supported its monastic communities, and continued the historic ministries of AROY, ARFORA, ARCOLA and the Department of Religious Education.

His Eminence is the author of the Episcopate's annual Paschal and Nativity Pastoral Letters, in which he addresses the clergy, monastics and faithful of the diocese with teaching grounded in the Scriptures, the Holy Fathers and the witness of the saints.

His Grace

Bishop Andrei (Hoarște) — Vicar Bishop

On Saturday, February 1, 2025, the Episcopate received its new Vicar Bishop in a hierarchal celebration at the Vatra Românească. Archimandrite Andrei (Hoarște) was ordained Auxiliary Bishop of the Romanian Orthodox Episcopate of America in the presence of clergy, monastics and faithful from across the diocese.

As Vicar Bishop, His Grace assists Archbishop Nathaniel in the archpastoral care of the parishes, monastic communities and ministries of the Episcopate, in canonical communion with the Orthodox Church in America.
