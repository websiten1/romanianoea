Structure of the Episcopate

Administration

Chancery Office

The Chancery Office at the Vatra Românească coordinates the day-to-day administration of the diocese, supporting the Archbishop, the Episcopate Council and parish life across North America.

Episcopate Council

The Episcopate Council is the standing administrative body of the diocese between Congresses. It is composed of clergy and lay delegates and meets regularly to address pastoral, financial and administrative matters.

Deaneries

The Episcopate is organized into deaneries that group neighboring parishes for pastoral care and shared ministry. The Romanian Orthodox Vicariate of Canada serves the parishes located in Canada.
