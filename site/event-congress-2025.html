93rd Episcopate Congress

Chicago, Illinois · 4–6 September 2025

Clergy & Laity Congress

Three days of prayer, reflection and common counsel

Delegates, clergy and faithful from across the United States and Canada gathered in Chicago, Illinois, 4–6 September 2025, for the 93rd Clergy & Laity Congress of the Romanian Orthodox Episcopate of America.

Opening · Thursday 4 September

The Congress opened with the celebration of the Divine Liturgy and a moleben for the work of the assembly, served by His Eminence Archbishop Nathaniel, together with His Grace Bishop Andrei and the clergy of the Episcopate.

The opening session included the registration of parish delegates, greetings from sister jurisdictions, and the Archbishop's opening address setting the tone of the Congress: gratitude for what has been received, honesty about the present, and hope for what God is preparing.

Friday 5 September — the working day

  • Presentation of the annual report of the Episcopate Council
  • Financial report and adoption of the next year's budget
  • Report of the Vatra Generations capital campaign
  • Reports from AROY, ARFORA, ARCOLA and the Religious Education Department
  • Reports from the Episcopate's monastic communities
  • Discussion sessions by region and by ministry

Saturday 6 September — decisions and doxology

The final day saw the adoption of resolutions, the confirmation of departmental leadership, and the reception of reports. The Congress closed with a service of thanksgiving and the Archbishop's closing word, sending delegates back to their parishes for a renewed year of ministry.

Host parish and hospitality

The Episcopate extends warm thanks to the host parish and the priests and faithful of the Chicago area for the hospitality, the liturgical beauty of the services and the warm Romanian welcome extended to all delegates.
